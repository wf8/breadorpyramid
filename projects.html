Phylogeny and divergence of North American Rhodiola

I've been researching the phylogeny and divergence times of the North American Rhodiola (Crassulaceae) using both nuclear and chloroplast DNA. I am particularly interested in using phylogenomic approaches to resolve the history of hybridization within plant groups. Restoration Map

Working with non-profit and volunteer conservation groups, I have developed open source web-based decision support tools to help document and plan ecological restoration projects. I'm interested in the ways information technology can be used to make spatially explicit conservation management more collaborative and transparent. Can we build useful tools for conservationists that incorporate metrics of phylogenetic diversity?
